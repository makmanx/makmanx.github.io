Research Interests

My research lies in the interface of harmonic analysis, elliptic and parabolic Partial Differential Equations, Potential Theory, and Geometric Measure Theory. In particular, I am interested in
  • Variational methods for linear and quasi-linear elliptic and parabolic PDEs.
  • Regularity and free boundary regularity for linear and quasi-linear elliptic and parabolic PDEs in non-smooth domains.
  • Conformal densities and metric properties of harmonic, $p$-harmonic, and elliptic measures.
  • Brunn-Minkowski type inequalities associated to quasi-linear elliptic PDEs and stability of these inequalities.
  • Minkowski type problems associated to quasi-linear elliptic PDEs.
