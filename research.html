Research Interests

My research lies in the interface of Analysis, Partial Differential Equations, Potential Theory, and Geometric Measure Theory. My interests are
  1. Variational methods for quasi-linear elliptic PDEs.
  2. Regularity and free boundary regularity for quasi-linear elliptic PDEs in non-smooth domains.
  3. Conformal densities and metric properties of harmonic, $p$-harmonic, and elliptic measures.
  4. Brunn-Minkowski type inequalities associated to quasi-linear elliptic PDEs and stability of these inequalities.
  5. Minkowski type problems associated to quasi-linear elliptic PDEs.
I defended my thesis in May 2014. Title: On the Hausdorff dimension of a certain measure arising from a positive weak solution to a divergence type PDE.
