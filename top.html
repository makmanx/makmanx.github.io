I am currently a lecturer (assistant professor) at the Department of Mathematical Sciences (DMS) at the University of Essex Colchester, UK. My research focuses on analysis of linear and nonlinear elliptic PDEs (Laplacian and p-Laplacian, and A-harmonic PDEs) and I am a member of the Analysis and Mathematical Physics research theme of the DMS.

My research lies in the interface of Analysis, Partial Differential Equations, Potential Theory, and Geometric Measure Theory. My research is supported by EPSRC's New Investigator Award. In particular, I am interested in
  • Variational methods for quasi-linear elliptic PDEs.
  • Regularity and free boundary regularity for quasi-linear elliptic PDEs in non-smooth domains.
  • Conformal densities and metric properties of harmonic, $p$-harmonic, and elliptic measures.
  • Brunn-Minkowski type inequalities associated to quasi-linear elliptic PDEs and stability of these inequalities.
  • Minkowski type problems associated to quasi-linear elliptic PDEs.
