I am currently a lecturer (assistant professor) at the Department of Mathematical Sciences (DMS) at the University of Essex Colchester, UK. My research focuses on analysis of linear and nonlinear elliptic PDEs (Laplacian and p-Laplacian, and A-harmonic PDEs) and I am a member of the Analysis and Mathematical Physics research theme of the DMS.

My research will be supported by the EPSRC New Investigator award Scheme. There will be a 2-year postdoc position (available from 30th of September 2022 until the 29th of September 2024) to work on problems related to linear and non-linear elliptic PDEs (Laplace and p-Laplace equations) and their applications in Minkowski-type problems and Brunn-Minkowski-type inequalities.
