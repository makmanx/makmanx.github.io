I am currently a lecturer (assistant professor) at University of Essex Colchester, UK. My research focuses on analysis of linear and nonlinear elliptic PDEs (Laplacian and p-Laplacian, and A-harmonic PDEs).

My research will be supported by the EPSRC New Investigator award Scheme. There will be a 2-year postdoc position starting in September/October 2022 to work on problems related to linear and non-linear elliptic PDEs (Laplace and p-Laplace equations) and their applications in Minkowski-type problems and Brunn-Minkowski-type inequalities. The job will be advertised soon. Please contact me directly (murat.akman@essex.ac.uk) if you have any questions.

I will co-organize (with George Papamikoss) a 2-day meeting on Integrability and Analysis of PDEs . This workshop is partially supported by the London Mathematical Society Scheme 9 Grant and by the Department of Mathematical Sciences of the University of Essex. There will be limited fund for travel and accommodation to participate in the workshop. The deadline for funding is 31st of May, 2022.
