I am currently a senior lecturer (assistant professor) at the School of Mathematics, Statistics and Actuarial Science (SMSAS) at the University of Essex Colchester, UK and I am a member of the Analysis and Mathematical Physics research theme of the SMSAS.

My research lies in the interface of harmonic analysis, elliptic and parabolic Partial Differential Equations, Potential Theory, and Geometric Measure Theory. My research is supported by EPSRC's New Investigator Award. In particular, I am interested in
  • Variational methods for linear and quasi-linear elliptic and parabolic PDEs.
  • Regularity and free boundary regularity for linear and quasi-linear elliptic and parabolic PDEs in non-smooth domains.
  • Conformal densities and metric properties of harmonic, $p$-harmonic, and elliptic measures.
  • Brunn-Minkowski type inequalities associated to quasi-linear elliptic PDEs and stability of these inequalities.
  • Minkowski type problems associated to quasi-linear elliptic PDEs.
