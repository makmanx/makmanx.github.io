PhD students

I am actively looking for PhD students to supervise on problems related to elliptic PDEs and their applications to funcational inequalities like Brunn-Minkowski inequalities and Minkowski type problems. These positions are subject to obtaining suitable funding.

There are funding bodies that I have applied in the past to supervise PhD students at Essex and if you are interested to apply for one of these scholarship to do PhD, contact me directly.
  • PhD Scholarships from the University of Essex.
  • Commonwealth PhD Scholarships are for candidates from least developed countries.
  • The Marshall Scholarships for students from the USA.

Currently I am looking for a 2-year Postdoc (available from 30th of September 2022 until the 29th of September 2024) to work on problems related to linear and non-linear elliptic PDEs (Laplace and p-Laplace equations) and their applications in Minkowski-type problems and Brunn-Minkowski-type inequalities.
